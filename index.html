> 10 PRINT "About Me"

I am a skilled professional educated & trained in computer programing and laboratory research; with a focus in cellular biology & chemical management.

My professional training began at Purdue University, graduating with a BSc in Cellular, Molecular, and Developmental Biology. I furthermore completed a MSc, at Purdue University, with a focus in Immunology & Genetics. Most recently, I serve as the Chemistry Department Laboratory Supervisor at Northern Michigan University, located in Marquette, Michigan.

Throughout my youth and professional path I have used computers to facilitate projects, increase my efficacy, and to have fun! My computer programing experience began with BASIC on a TI-99/4a computer that my uncle gave me. A few years down the road, I built my first DX2 486 computer and got to experience the wonders of a GUI; DOS 6.22, Windows 3.11 and a VGA graphics card had me off to the races. I began learning C, in middle school; incrementally adding Python, R, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP to my repertoire.
